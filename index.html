About the Author

Hi, I’m Nidhi S—a quiet soul who feels deeply and writes from the tender spaces where emotions often go unheard.

She Deserves the Best isn’t just a story—it’s a piece of my soul, stitched with soft ache, silent strength, and healing hope.

I write for the ones who cry quietly, love loudly, and carry storms behind calm smiles.

For every girl who ever felt invisible and still chose to rise—this book is for you.

If my words find you, hold you, or help you breathe a little easier… then they’ve done what they came to do. 💫

You can also share your story—
Who knows, it might become the heartbeat of my next novel.

Author's Note

She Deserves the Best is more than a novel— it's a piece of my heart, stitched with quiet ache, raw truths, and the soft courage to heal.

It was born from silent tears, late-night thoughts, and the unspoken battles so many carry within. Through Sanya’s journey, I wanted to give voice to the emotions we’re often told to hide— grief, loneliness, longing, and the soft magic of friendship.

To every girl who has ever felt unseen, unheard, or unloved: this story is for you.

You deserve to be held. You deserve to be heard. You deserve to be loved— gently, wholly, and without conditions.

Thank you for picking up this book. I hope it feels like a warm hug on your hardest days.

With love,
Nidhi S
